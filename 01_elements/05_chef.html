Chiken Biryani

🌾 Ingredients

🐔 For Chicken Marinade:

  • 500g chicken (bone-in preferred)
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1½ tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • A handful of mint and coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 2 sliced green chilies (optional)

🍚 For Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 mins)
  • 5-6 cups water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3-4 cloves
  • 1-2 green cardamoms
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp salt

🧅 Other Ingredients:

  • 2 large onions (thinly sliced)
  • 3 tbsp oil or ghee
  • A pinch of saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk (optional)
  • 1 tsp kewra water or rose water (optional)

🔥 Cooking Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

  1. In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, salt, mint, coriander, and green chilies.
  2. Add chicken pieces and mix well.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best flavor).

Step 2: Prepare the Rice

  1. Boil water in a large pot. Add bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, and salt.
  2. Add soaked rice and cook until 70-80% done (grains should break with a bite).
  3. Drain the rice and set aside.

Step 3: Fry Onions

  1. In a deep pan or biryani pot, heat oil/ghee.
  2. Fry sliced onions until golden brown and crispy.
  3. Take out half the onions and set aside for layering.

Step 4: Layering and Cooking

  1. In the same pot with half the onions, spread the marinated chicken evenly.
  2. Spread half the cooked rice over the chicken.
  3. Add some fried onions, mint, coriander, saffron milk, and kewra/rose water.
  4. Layer the remaining rice and repeat the toppings.

Step 5: Dum Cooking (Slow Steam)

  1. On Stove: Cover the pot with a tight lid or seal with dough. Cook on medium-high for 5-7 mins, then on low flame for 20-25 mins.
